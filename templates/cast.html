Main Cast

Sacha Baron Cohen

as Ali G / Borat

Sacha Baron Cohen portrays the titular character Ali G, a wannabe gangster from Staines, as well as a brief appearance as Borat Sagdiyev.

Michael Gambon

as Prime Minister

Michael Gambon plays the Prime Minister, who becomes unexpectedly popular after Ali G joins his team.

Charles Dance

as David Carlton

Charles Dance portrays the villainous Chancellor David Carlton, who plots to overthrow the Prime Minister.

Martin Freeman

as Ricky C

Martin Freeman plays Ricky C, Ali's best friend and a member of his gang, the "West Staines Massiv".

Rhona Mitra

as Kate Hedges

Rhona Mitra portrays Kate Hedges, a political aide who works closely with Ali G after he joins the government.
