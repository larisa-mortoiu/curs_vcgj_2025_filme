About JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hirohiko Araki. The story follows the supernatural adventures of the Joestar family across generations, with each part featuring a different descendant of the family as the protagonist, each nicknamed "JoJo."

The series is famous for its unique art style, creative battles, memorable characters, and its signature "Stands" - manifestations of psychic power that represent a user's fighting spirit and have various abilities.
